**Overview**  
Analyzed telecom customer churn using Excel dashboards to uncover actionable patterns in demographics, services, and contract types. Delivered insights to support retention strategies and business decision-making.

**Tools & Techniques**  
- Excel: Pivot Tables, Slicers, Graphs, Dashboards  
- Formulas: COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation: Formatting, Filtering, Metric Calculations  

**Key Analytical Insights**  
- **Customer Segments at Risk:** Senior citizens and customers without dependents or partners  
- **Service & Contract Impact:** Month-to-month contracts and electronic check payments show highest churn  
- **Service Gaps:** Fiber optic users lacking tech support or online security are most likely to churn  
- **Minimal Factors:** Gender did not significantly affect churn  

**Dashboards & Interactivity**  
- Overview: Total churn and trends over tenure  
- Demographics: Age, partner, and dependent status segmentation  
- Services: Internet type, contract, and payment method impact  
- **Interactive slicers** allow dynamic exploration of high-risk segments  

**Relevant Takeaways**  
- Identify priority segments for **targeted retention campaigns**  
- Drive **product and service enhancements** based on observed churn patterns  
- Support **strategic business decisions** with clear, data-driven visualizations
